Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Overview

Gainday is an investment portfolio tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

All your portfolio data, including accounts, holdings, and transaction records, is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your financial data on any server.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Account credentials or passwords
  • Financial account numbers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

Third-Party Services

Gainday uses the following third-party services to provide market data:

  • Yahoo Finance API — to fetch real-time stock quotes, historical prices, and currency exchange rates.

These requests contain only stock ticker symbols and do not include any personal or financial data. Please refer to Yahoo's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode and encryption.

Children's Privacy

Gainday is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: gainday.app@gmail.com
